Mar 20

Pre-State Convention Hype Meeting

We taught members about State Convention and our cheers. After shouting our lungs out, we had nutella breadsticks to share.

Feb 13

Valentine's Day Meeting

In our special Valentine's Day meeting, we rated Greco-Romano figures based on who has the least rizz! The winner (or loser) was Polyphemus.

Dec 19

Saturnalia Party

We had a Saturnalia party hosted in our Latin classroom! We hung out and talked, played hangman with Latin words and phrases, and ate pizza and donuts.

Nov 21

Thanksgiving feast

We gathered for this meeting for a Thanksgiving dinner while we discussed types of meals and how they were eaten in Ancient Rome. The food did not last very long...

Nov 7

Gladiator Meeting

Members fashioned swords out of duct tape and foam then took to the arenas to battle! Spectators voted on whether to kill or spare defeated gladiators.

Oct 24

Halloween Meeting

In this meeting, we celebrated Halloween by painting pumpkins, dressing up in costumes, and participating in trivia for candy!

Oct 18

Fall Forum Preparation

After getting kicked out of Ms. Eller's room and being relocated to Mr. Conlan's (the AP Lit teacher) room, we had our Fall Forum preparations and drafted our horse banner for the Fall Forum contest.

Oct 10

Fall Forum Information Meeting

We dedicated this meeting to providing information on the events and our itinerary for Fall Forum, hosted by our one and only Andrew Lee!

Sep 12

Chubby Bunny Meeting

We played Chubby Bunny, a game to see who could fit the most marshmallows in their mouth while saying "penguis cunniculus"(Chubby Bunny in Latin).

Aug 29

Introduction Meeting

In our first meeting of the year, we welcomed new members, gave a quick introduction to JCL, and introduced the officers for the 24-25 school year! We also had ice cream snacks we gave out to everyone and took an amazing group photo!
