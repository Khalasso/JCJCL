Fall Forum

2025's Fall Forum was a great time for Johns Creek! Joshua Li, our 2nd VP, led us in spirit for the very first time, earning JCHS first place in spirit for small chapters. Other than spirit, our members were spread out across academic ventures, athletic endeavors, and more. Overall, Johns Creek earned 1st in spirit, 3rd in basketball, 1st and 5th in level 2 sightreading, and 3rd in the 50-yard dash.

October 25th @ Northview High School

Saturnalia Party

Johns Creek hosted this year's Saturnalia party on December 11th, inviting members from Chatahoochee, Northview, and Innovation Academy. During our Saturnalia party, we had a karaoke party and organized gift bags that were donated to orphans at the Roswell Presbyterian Church for the 2025 Toyland charity drive. Thanks to the efforts of all 4 schools, we donated a combined 70 gift bags!

December 11th @ Johns Creek High School

Middle School Visit

The officer board from Johns Creek High School took a field trip to Autrey Mill Middle School to represent Latin and introduce information on language pathways in high school. With the Spanish and French club, the JCL board presented information about each high school language and their respective clubs and honors societies.
