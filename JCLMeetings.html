StateCon Prep & Ultimate Frisbee

We started the meeting by reintroducing the upcoming State Convention and registering members for the event. We then went out to the courtyard to play Ultimate Frisbee!

Feb 12

Ancient Myth Figures Dating Game

For our Valentine's special, we played a game of Kiss, Marry, Kill with ancient figures from mythology! We took votes of 20 different figures and ultimately determined the most attractive ancient myth figure is the Hydra! (Our chapter is full of freaks)

Jan 29

Debate Meeting

Our JCL Members became the ancient Roman senate as they debated issues that would have been hot topics of debate in ancient Rome. Members took on different personalities, such as the Suicidal Pessimist, the Party Animal, and the Master of Insults.

Jan 15

Trojan Horse Pretzels

Memberes learned about the Trojan Horse and how it was employed in the Trojan War to secure a victory for the Greeks. Members then proceeded to forms groups and construct their own Trojan Horses out of marshmallows and toothpicks.

Nov 6

Fall Forum Recap + Omens

We taught our members about ancient Roman augury and other common omens. To demonstrate this, we sacraficed a cow puppet and read its organs to determine whether we got a good or bad fortune, and then proceeded to feast on its organs! (Don't worry, the organs were Jolly Ranchers)

Oct 23

Last Fall Forum Prep

We finalized our plans for Fall Forum and ran through spirit one last time. We created props for spirit as well, and hyped everyone up for the event!

Oct 9

Fall Forum Spirit & Charades

We introduced Fall Forum and Joshua Li, our 2nd VP, took our members through an overview of spirit, practicing chants to perform at Fall Forum.
