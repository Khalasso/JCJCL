National Latin Exam

What is the NLE?

The NLE, or National Latin Exam, is a test to prove your Latin knowledge against students across the country. Each year, students can take one of the exams offered by the NLE, ranging from Introduction to Latin to Advanced Latin Reading Comprehension.

Seniors who score a gold medal on the advanced tests are eligible for scholarships. It's a great way to test your Latin skills and earn awards and bragging rights! Check out the official NLE website below for more information on the different exams offered, how to register, and how to prepare.
