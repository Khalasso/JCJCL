Local / Regional

Fall Forum

Fall Forum offers many testing opportunities for members to showcase their knowledge and skills. Testing events include topics about Latin grammar, derivatives, prose/poetry, Ancient Geography, mythology, and more!

Key Testing Events:

  • Advanced Prose
  • Latin Derivatives
  • Greek Derivatives
  • Ancient Geography
MAR
State-Wide

GJCL State Convention

The biggest state event of the year! Much like Fall Forum, State Convention testing events encompass a wide array of tests ranging from Latin grammar to Ancient Geography and Roman History.

Key Testing Events:

  • Latin Grammar
  • Advanced Poetry
  • Mythology
  • Ancient Geography
  • Academic Decathlon
JUL
National

NJCL National Convention

Testing events at National Convention are somewhat similar to those at State Convention and Fall Forum. Members who score very well may be eligible for cash prizes and scholarships!

Key Testing Events:

  • National Classical Etymology
  • National Roman Civilization
  • National Latin Vocabulary
  • National Hellenic Civilization
