What is Certamen?

"Certamen", or competition in Latin, is a Quizbowl-style competition where you can test your knowledge of Latin literature and grammar, as well as Roman history and mythology, against other teams. In Certamen, 3 teams of 4 race to buzz in and answer questions pertaining to these topics. If a player answers correctly, their team has an opportunity to answer two bonus questions, earning them extra points, but an incorrect answer will give the other teams a chance to buzz and steal. The team with the most points at the end of the round wins.

Why you should join Certamen

Certamen is a great way to test your skill on the Latin language and culture. You get the opportunity to compete head-to-head with so many people who are just as passionate of the classics. Additionally, the bonds you create with your team and even your opponents are invaluable. Being a relatively-small community, everyone in Certamen knows each other well, and we are all helping each grow. Try it out, you won't regret it!
