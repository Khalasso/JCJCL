Welcome to our Chapter!

Salvete omnes! Welcome to the Johns Creek Junior Classical League, a chapter in Johns Creek, Georgia expressing their strong interest in Latin. We are passionate about Latin and the Classics, and we are a close group of friends happy to be in each other's company celebrating what we love. We are a proud chapter of the Georgia Junior Classical League and National Junior Classical League.
